Stoneman's Cabin

Creation Story: "While it’s especially difficult to be anywhere that we wish to be this year, I hope to share this scene with viewers so they can see VR-Art is a powerful way to travel. And most importantly, it allows us to share our favorite memories with others. We could illustrate and be in any place that we wish to be whether it’s from our memory or from our dreams."
