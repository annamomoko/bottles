About

The project was created during Covid from 2020 to 2021

The start: In a chat with friends at work, I realize how different each of us felt about the impact of global pandemic. Even though seemingly experiencing the same reality, yet we also have so many unique stories from our perspectives.
And from that discussion, in the moment of isolated in each home, I thought, what if we create a VR cultural museum to share how each of us felt?

So that "This is my story" came alive - it's a virtual cultural museum that brings diverse arts together during Covid time. It's a place for people to share their unique story from a cultural point of view.
