"Intersection" at Palo Alto Code:ART Festival 2023 October


Intersection was an immersive light- and sound-based multimedia art installation where an underwater forest moved into a downtown alley. The visitor was invited into a multisensory dream world where hidden messages from the sea were uncovered.

Intersection provided a place to pause, reflect, and feel, inviting the visitor on a journey of discovery between the artificial and natural, self and community, past and future.

Walking through the seaweed forest, to experience the unique soundscape, can you hear what ocean is talking about?
